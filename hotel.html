Luxury Stay Hotel - Gjirokastër

About the Hotel

Luxury Stay Hotel is a charming boutique hotel in the heart of Gjirokastër, offering guests a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional Albanian hospitality.

Accommodation

The hotel features beautifully designed rooms that capture the essence of Gjirokastër’s historic architecture while providing modern amenities for a comfortable stay.

Hotel Features

  • Spacious and elegantly furnished rooms with stunning city views.
  • Authentic Albanian and Mediterranean cuisine served in the on-site restaurant.
  • Rooftop terrace offering panoramic views of the castle and old town.
  • Complimentary Wi-Fi and 24-hour concierge service.
  • Close proximity to major attractions, including the Old Bazaar and Gjirokastër Castle.

Interesting Facts

The hotel is housed in a historic stone building, preserving the traditional Gjirokastër architectural style while integrating modern luxury.

Many rooms have handcrafted wooden furniture and traditional Albanian decor, offering an authentic experience.

Booking & Contact

The hotel offers online booking through its website and is open year-round. Special packages are available for long stays and group reservations.
